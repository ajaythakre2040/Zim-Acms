19. Data Relationships
Key Entity Relationships:

Sites contain Buildings, Zones, and Devices
Buildings contain Floors
Zones can be nested (parent-child) and linked to Buildings/Floors
Zones contain Doors
Doors have In/Out readers and Controllers (Devices)
People belong to Departments and have Designations
People have Credentials, Access Cards, and Shift assignments
Visitors have Visits linked to Sites and Hosts
Visits can have Access permissions to specific Doors
Attendance records link People, Sites, Shifts, and Devices
Access Logs/Events track People, Visitors, Devices, Sites, Zones, and Doors
